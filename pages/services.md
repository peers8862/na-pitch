NAC operates across three integrated service pillars, each reinforcing the others and generating compounding value for clients and partners.

## 1. Precision Assembly

End-to-end small-batch PCB and electronics assembly in a 4,000–8,000 sq. ft. Hamilton facility built to IPC-A-610 and related standards. Services span component sourcing, SMT and through-hole assembly, automated optical inspection, functional test, and commercial-grade packaging. Our facility is designed for rapid-turnaround prototype runs as well as consistent small-series production for industrial and commercial clients.

## 2. Firmware & Binary Optimization

NAC's differentiated technical capability lies in low-level firmware engineering: assembly language, binary-level optimization, and embedded systems configuration tailored to edge and industrial computing deployments. For clients in industrial IoT, field instrumentation, and portable work equipment, this translates to dramatically lower power consumption, faster execution, longer device lifespans, and reduced hardware costs.

## 3. Workforce Education & Ecosystem Development

NAC operates technical training programs that feed both our own production floor and the broader regional ecosystem. Curriculum spans PCB assembly, embedded systems programming, quality assurance, and industrial automation — delivered in partnership with post-secondary institutions in both Ontario and New York State. This pillar ensures a self-replenishing talent pipeline and positions NAC as a civic and economic anchor in every geography it operates.
