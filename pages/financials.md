## Startup Capital Requirements

| Category | Estimated Amount |
|---|---|
| Facility build-out & leasehold improvements | $60,000–$90,000 |
| Assembly & test equipment | $80,000–$110,000 |
| IT infrastructure & network systems | $18,000–$25,000 |
| Initial component inventory & tooling | $20,000–$30,000 |
| Working capital reserve (6 months) | $60,000–$80,000 |
| Legal, registration & professional fees | $15,000–$22,000 |
| Marketing & business development | $10,000–$15,000 |
| **Total Startup Capital Requirement** | **$279,000 (est.)** |

## Year-One Operating Costs

With four full-time employees and Hamilton premises at steady state, NAC projects approximately $29,000 per month in operating costs, totaling ~$628,000 for the first twelve months including startup capital deployment.

## Non-Repayable Grant Potential

Based on identified federal, provincial, and regional programs — including NRC IRAP, the Ontario Regional Development Program, and Fort Erie's Gateway Community Improvement Plan — NAC anticipates $100,000–$250,000 in non-repayable support during Year 1, significantly reducing the effective net capital requirement for investors. NYPA hydropower cost reductions and START-UP NY tax abatements provide ongoing operational savings on the US side.
